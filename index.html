INFORMATION:

Do you only make scripts for QBCore/QBox?

While I primarily develop and test my scripts using QBCore and QBox, I’ve been actively working toward making all of my projects fully framework-agnostic. Through the use of community_bridge, I’m converting my systems to be compatible across multiple frameworks, inventories, targeting systems, and banking solutions. My long-term goal is to ensure that every release is flexible, scalable, and easy to integrate into any server environment—without forcing owners to restructure their entire setup.

Are all your scripts free?

While I currently have plenty of projects that are completely free and open-source, most of what I’m building right now is part of my experimentation and learning process. Script development is something I genuinely enjoy as a hobby in my spare time, and it allows me to explore new ideas without pressure. I do have a few paid scripts in development, but I plan to focus on selling once I’m confident I can consistently deliver bigger, better, and more polished releases.
